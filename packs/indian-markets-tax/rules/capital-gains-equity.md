**Rule (external):** For listed Indian equity / equity mutual funds, the holding
period splits gains into **Short-Term (STCG)** — held ≤ 12 months — and
**Long-Term (LTCG)** — held > 12 months. The cutoff is measured from the
acquisition date to the date of transfer.

**Source:** Income-tax Act, sections 111A (STCG on STT-paid equity) and 112A
(LTCG on STT-paid equity). Verify current rates/exemption thresholds against the
prevailing Finance Act before relying on a number in code.

**Applies to:** any P&L / realised-gains computation that classifies a sale by
tax treatment. A holding-period boundary off by a day mis-buckets the gain — make
the comparison inclusive of exactly 12 months as STCG.
